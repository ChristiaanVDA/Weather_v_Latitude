Latitude vs Weather

About this Project

The Latitude vs Weather project was a simple exercise to investigate how (or if) certain elements of weather are affected by latitude. For instance, temperature should increase as one approaches the equator, or decrease as one approaches the poles. But what about humidty, precipitation, or wind speed? Data Sources

Latitudes and longitudes were calculated randomly, then matched to the nearest city using CityPy. Current weather data for those cities was obtained from OpenWeatherMap on February 22, 2018 at approximately 10:00 AM GMT.
