Summary: Lattitude vs Weather

The Latitude vs Weather project was a simple exercise to investigate how (or if) certain elements of weather are affected by latitude. For instance, temperature should increase as one approaches the equator, or decrease as one approaches the poles. But what about humidty, precipitation, or wind speed? Please explore these pages to see the results.
