Summary: Lattitude vs Weather

The Latitude vs Weather project was a simple exercise to investigate how (or if) certain elements of weather are affected by latitude. For instance, temperature should increase as one approaches the equator and decrease as one approaches the poles. But what about humidty, precipitation, or wind speed?

Latitudes and longitudes were calculated randomly, then matched to the nearest city using CityPy. Over 500 unique cities were obtained this way. Then, current weather data for each city was obtained from OpenWeatherMap. Weather data was obtained on February 22, 2018 at approximately 10:00 AM GMT.
