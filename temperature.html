Temperature

As one would expect, there is a strong correlation between latitude and temperature. Temperatures are at their highest near the equator, then decline as latitudes move towards the poles. Note the effect in the dataset is much more pronounced in the Northern Hemisphere. This is likely due to seasonality when the data was measured. The Southern Hemisphere was in summer, leading to higher temperatures and less contrast with temperatures at the equator. The Northern Hemisphere was in winter, resulting in more a more extreme drop off as we move northward.
